Difference between wait()  and sleep() Methods

|----------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|
|          wait()										   |						sleep()					|
|----------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|
|1. wait() forcefully exits the thread to leave the obj.   | 1. sleep() halts for given number of seconds 	|
|2. it is instance method								   | 2. it is static method							|
|3. It is for the inter-thread communications			   | 3. It is "NOT" for inter-thread communication  |
|----------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------------|
